Title: Fire Breathing
Medium: Webcam, computer (wired or wireless), 55-inch or larger monitor, portable white backdrop, MoveNet body-pose system
Dimensions: Variable (depending on installation environment)
Description (English):
Fire Breathing is an interactive media installation that uses MoveNet body-pose tracking to follow the viewer’s nose. Standing before a portable white background, participants allow the webcam to capture their movement; the system then draws a red line in real time based on the motion of their nose, accompanied by a mirrored, pulsating line that behaves like an organic, breathing creature.
Drawing with the nose removes artistic pretense and replaces it with playfulness, immediacy, and joyful bodily engagement. In this informal collaboration, participants co-create with the algorithm, experiencing a light, dynamic exchange between technology, movement, and improvisation.
The work invites both artists and non-artists to collaborate with machine-learning technologies, offering a simple and joyful dance between human motion and computational response.

名称：火气旺
媒介（Medium）： 摄像头、计算机（无线或有线连接）、55 英寸或更大显示器、可移动白色背景、MoveNet 姿态识别系统
尺寸（Dimensions）： 可变尺寸（依展示空间而定）
作品描述（Description）：
《火气旺》是一件互动式新媒体装置，通过 MoveNet 人体姿态识别技术追踪观众的鼻子位置。当参与者站在白色背景前，摄像头捕捉其动作，系统实时绘制一条跟随鼻子移动的红色轨迹，同时生成一条镜像的动态线条，像有机生物般脉动、呼吸。
这种以鼻子作画的方式消除了艺术创作中的矫饰与距离，替代之的是一种轻松、趣味与即兴的身体参与。观众在毫无负担的状态下，与算法共同创作，体验技术、身体与动作之间的嬉戏性互动。
作品希望为艺术家与非艺术家提供共同合作的可能——通过简单的动作，与机器学习技术展开一场愉快、生动、非正式的舞蹈。
